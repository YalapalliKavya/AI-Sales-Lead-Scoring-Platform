About the Platform

The AI Sales Lead Scoring Platform is an enterprise-level system designed to help organizations prioritize potential customers by predicting which leads are most likely to convert into paying clients. It combines machine learning with CRM-style workflows to improve sales efficiency, reduce manual effort, and support data-driven decision-making.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help sales teams identify the right opportunities at the right time by using intelligent lead scoring, centralized lead management, and clear analytics dashboards.

AI Lead Scoring

Predict lead conversion probability using machine learning models such as Logistic Regression and XGBoost.

Multi-User CRM

Support admins and multiple sales users with role-based access, lead assignment, and user-specific dashboards.

Analytics Dashboard

Monitor lead quality, conversion rate, source analytics, and sales pipeline performance in one place.

Technology Stack

The platform is built using Django for backend development, HTML/CSS/JavaScript for frontend, MySQL for database management, and machine learning models for predictive lead analysis.
